About

The Salary Timer Calculator lets you see how much you earn over different periods of time. It allows you to convert your annual salary or hourly salary to see how much you earn in a second, a minute, a day, weeks,etc. It’s main feature is to calculate your earnings for every second in a given number of hours. To see how much you are earning in a day’s worth of work, all live and quickly, saving you time and effort.
